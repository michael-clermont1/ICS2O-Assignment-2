Jeff Dean

    
Lead of Googles AI   Software Engineer   Computer Scientist

    
    
"You get up and you're only 30 feet from work."

Jeff dean was born on july 1968. He is currently a software enginners and
computer scientist and is the lead of googles AI divison. He
recieved a B.S from the university of Minnesota for computer
science. Before joining Google, he was at DEC/Compaq's Western
Research Laboratory,where he worked on profiling tools, microprocessor
architecture, and information retrieval. Much of his work was
completed in close collaboration with Sanjay Ghemawat. He joinedgoogle
in mid 1999. After he joined google he designed a lot of googles large
advertisments and query service systems. He also worked on various internal
software development tools and has had significant involvement in the
engineering hiring process.
